ABOUT ...

Born and raised in Minas Gerais until the age of 18, with a childhood and adolescence in a small town. At 18 I had my first experience of a big city, I lived for almost a year in Campinas in the interior of São Paulo, working as a bartender at the Conceição snack bar, where I sold the best 'coxinha' I've ever eaten; I returned home, but then I already missed the buildings and the atmosphere of the big city. I went back!. I went to the city of Americana, and soon after I came to São Paulo, where I live to this day.

Here, I worked for a few years at a baby stroller importer for 3 years, and then another 17 at Multfoco, an importer of optical products, where I grew up and specialized professionally as a technician in equipment maintenance. I worked for another 5 years in the optics industry as a technology representative in lens production and lens sales. I specialized in electronics at 27 years old. At 37, I wanted to go to college in the IT field, but after 6 months I gave up, seeing that the degree wouldn't give me enough knowledge to enter a new profession as I wanted. So I moved on with my life, with my work, many trips across the country and some to Spain..

At 47 things changed a little, and now writing this article, I realized that my life goes in cycles every 10 years that change something... now determined to follow a new career, but, I confess that I had a lot of difficulty at the beginning , this area is an ocean of information that it is impossible to learn everything, I realized this and stopped wanting to know everything, it would take many lives and I don't know if a human being would be capable.

So since mid-2022, I started my training in software engineering and other courses related to Front End development.

Well, that was a little and summarized the story of my life. Thank you for your attention.
